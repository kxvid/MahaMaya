Measure JoyReviving Y2K Tech.

How a curated vintage camera brand escaped eBay's seller fees and Shopify's limitations — and built a custom commerce engine that puts them in full control of their brand, margins, and growth.

Client Measure Joy
Industry Vintage Tech / E-Commerce
Track B — Commerce Engine
Platform Custom Build (Next.js)

The Problem

Great product. Wrong infrastructure.

Measure Joy curates, restores, and sells vintage Y2K digital cameras — the kind of point-and-shoots that have become cultural artifacts. Every camera goes through a 12-point inspection. Every unit ships with a 90-day warranty. The product is premium. The experience should be too.

But the business was trapped on eBay — hemorrhaging revenue to seller fees, competing with low-quality listings, and building someone else's brand equity with every sale. The obvious alternative was Shopify, but for a boutique operation selling curated inventory (not mass-produced SKUs), Shopify's monthly fees, transaction costs, and theme limitations didn't make economic sense.

Measure Joy needed something different. A platform that matched the quality of their product — without the recurring platform tax.

💰

eBay Seller Fees

12.9% final value fees on every sale, plus insertion fees and promoted listing costs. On a $120 camera, eBay takes roughly $15 before shipping. That's margin evaporating on every transaction.

🏫

Shopify Overhead

$39/mo minimum for a real store, plus 2.9% + 30¢ per transaction (or more without Shopify Payments). Theme customization is limited. Apps add cost. For a lean boutique operation, it's death by a thousand subscriptions.

🚫

Zero Brand Control

On eBay, your brand is a listing among millions. On Shopify, your brand is a template among thousands. Neither platform lets you build the kind of immersive, curated shopping experience that vintage tech buyers expect.

The Solution

A custom commerce engine.

We built Measure Joy a fully custom e-commerce platform from the ground up. No themes. No marketplace middlemen. A bespoke storefront designed specifically for the curated vintage tech experience — fast, branded, and owned entirely by the business.

Custom Next.js Storefront

Server-side rendered, lightning-fast product pages. No theme bloat. Every pixel designed around the vintage camera browsing experience — from category filtering to product detail galleries.

Direct Payment Integration

Stripe API integration with no platform middleman. Lower transaction fees, direct payouts, and support for Apple Pay and Google Pay — maximizing conversion at checkout.

Managed Infrastructure

Premium hosting with edge caching for sub-2-second load times. SSL, security patches, and performance monitoring — all managed by us so the team can focus on sourcing and restoring cameras.

Customer Accounts & Auth

Secure authentication system so returning buyers can track orders, view purchase history, and check warranty status — building the kind of customer relationship eBay never allowed.

We were paying eBay thousands in fees every year and our customers didn't even know our brand name. Now we own the entire experience — and we keep the margin.
Christian Velasquez — Founder, Measure Joy
